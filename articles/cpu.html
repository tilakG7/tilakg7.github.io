Logic-Level Breadboard Computer

If you take the processor inside your phone, tablet or computer and "zoom in", this is the computer you get. Well at least functionally, it has all the principal components of a processor like the ALU, Registers and it has most of the fundamental electronic components - flip flops and logic gates.

What continually fascinates me about having built this computer is how it compares to a modern computer in its size -

A modern computer built at this scale would be larger in area than New York City.

Here are the specs of this computer:

  • 8-bit
  • Clock speed - 450Hz
  • 16 bytes RAM
  • No ROM

So nowhere near as powerful as what you would find in a modern computer - 64-bit, GHz clock speeds, GBs RAM and hunders of GB ROM.
